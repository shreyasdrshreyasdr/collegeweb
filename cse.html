Computer Science Engineering (CSE) subjects include foundational areas like Data Structures, Algorithms, Operating Systems, Computer Networks, and Databases, alongside core computer concepts such as Computer Architecture and Software Engineering. Students also study essential mathematics like Discrete Mathematics and Calculus, learn various Programming Languages, and can explore specialized electives in fields like Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Cybersecurity, and Cloud Computing.
