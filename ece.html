ECE (Electronics and Communication Engineering) subjects include core areas like Analog and Digital Electronics, Signals and Systems, Electromagnetic Theory, and Communication Systems, along with fundamental courses in Engineering Mathematics, Circuit Theory, and Microprocessors. Students also study specialized topics such as VLSI Design, Digital Signal Processing, Control Systems, and Internet of Things (IoT), with emerging technologies like Artificial Intelligence and 5G communication also forming part of the curriculum.
